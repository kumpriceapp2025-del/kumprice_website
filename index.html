System Overview

A capstone-developed system connecting local Small & Medium Enterprises (SMEs) with community consumers through a centralized mobile platform for real-time product and price management.

Project Objective

The project aims to develop a centralized mobile platform that allows local SMEs to digitally present their product prices and availability while enabling consumers to easily compare prices across nearby stores before making purchasing decisions.

Target Beneficiaries

Local SMEs benefit from increased digital visibility and easier product information management, while consumers gain access to real-time price comparison and product availability from participating neighborhood stores.

Real-world Impact

The system promotes price transparency, supports local businesses by increasing their digital presence, and empowers consumers to make more informed purchasing decisions.

Key System Functionalities

Comprehensive features addressing both SME management needs and consumer requirements

Centralized Mobile Platform

Store owners can add, update, and manage product listings including prices and availability, allowing consumers to view accurate product information across participating stores.

Real-Time Price Comparison

Consumers can compare product prices from multiple participating local stores in real time, helping them choose the most convenient and affordable option.

QR Code Integration

QR scanning allows users to instantly access store page for product details and price information within the application.

Location-Based Services

Consumers can locate nearby participating stores using GPS-based mapping and view available products and prices.

System Administration Dashboard

The administrative panel allows system administrators to manage registered stores, monitor product listings, and oversee overall platform activity.

Offline Sync

The application supports offline viewing of previously accessed product data and automatically synchronizes information once internet connectivity is restored.

Easy Installation

Get started in just a few simple steps

1
Download the APK File

Click the download button at the top of this page. The KumPrice application file (kumprice_1.0.2.apk, 141MB) will be saved to your device's Downloads folder. Wait for the download to complete before proceeding to the next step.

File size: 141MB only • No registration required • Safe and verified
2
Enable Unknown Sources Installation

Before installing, allow installations from unknown sources:

  • Open Settings on your Android device
  • Navigate to Security or Privacy
  • Find and enable "Install unknown apps" or "Unknown sources"
  • Select your browser or file manager and toggle it ON
Note: You can disable this setting after installation for security purposes
3
Install the Application

Locate the downloaded APK file in your Downloads folder or notification panel. Tap on kumprice_1.0.2.apk to begin installation. Review the permissions shown and tap Install. The installation process typically takes 10 to 30 seconds.

Important: If you see a Play Protect warning, tap "More details" then "Install anyway" - the app is safe and verified
4
Launch and Setup

After installation completes, tap Open or find the KumPrice icon in your app drawer. On first launch, you will be prompted to grant the following permissions:

  • Location Access: Required to find stores near your current location
  • Internet Access: Needed to fetch real-time prices and product availability
  • Storage Access: Allows caching of data for offline viewing
Once permissions are granted, you can immediately start searching for products and comparing prices

Troubleshooting Guide

Common installation issues and solutions

Installation Blocked

Problem: "For your security, your phone is not allowed to install unknown apps from this source."

Solution: Go to Settings → Security → Install unknown apps → Select your browser/file manager → Toggle "Allow from this source" ON.

App Not Installed

Problem: Installation fails with "App not installed" error.

Solution: Ensure you have enough storage space (at least 115MB free). Clear cache in Settings → Storage. Download the APK again if it was corrupted.

App Keeps Crashing

Problem: App crashes immediately after opening.

Solution: Clear app data in Settings → Apps → KumPrice → Storage → Clear Data. Restart your device. Ensure your Android version meets minimum requirements (6.0+).

Location Not Working

Problem: Store locator feature not finding nearby stores.

Solution: Enable Location/GPS in Settings. Grant location permission to KumPrice in Settings → Apps → KumPrice → Permissions → Location → Allow all the time or While using the app.

Unable to Load Data

Problem: Prices and availability not showing.

Solution: Check your internet connection. Ensure the app has internet permission. Try switching between WiFi and mobile data. Clear app cache and restart.

Security Warning

Problem: Google Play Protect warning during installation.

Solution: This is normal for apps not from Play Store. Tap "More details" → "Install anyway" if you trust the source. KumPrice is safe and scans are regularly performed.
