1. Getting Started

What is KumPrice?

KumPrice is a smart shopping companion app that helps you compare prices, check product availability, and find stores near you. Save time and money by making informed purchasing decisions with real-time data.

Key Features:
  • Real-time price comparison across multiple stores
  • Instant product availability checking
  • GPS-powered store locator
  • Price drop alerts and notifications
  • Offline data caching for quick access
  • User-friendly interface designed for speed

2. Installation Guide

1
Download the APK File

Click the download button on our website or use the direct link provided. The KumPrice.apk file will be saved to your device's Downloads folder.

2
Enable Installation from Unknown Sources

For Android 8.0 and above:

  • Go to Settings → Apps & Notifications
  • Tap "Special app access" → "Install unknown apps"
  • Select your browser or file manager
  • Toggle "Allow from this source" ON
3
Install the Application

Navigate to your Downloads folder and tap on KumPrice.apk. You may see a security warning - this is normal for apps not from Google Play Store. Tap "Install" and wait for the process to complete.

4
Launch and Setup

Once installed, tap "Open" or find the KumPrice icon in your app drawer. The app will request necessary permissions on first launch.

3. First Launch Setup

Required Permissions

When you first open KumPrice, you'll be asked to grant the following permissions:

Location Access

Required to find nearby stores and show accurate distances. You can choose "While using the app" or "All the time" based on your preference.

Internet Access

Needed to fetch real-time prices, product availability, and store information.

Storage Access

Allows the app to cache data for faster loading and offline viewing of previously searched products.

Notifications

Enable to receive price drop alerts and special deal notifications.

4. Searching for Products

How to Search
  1. Open the KumPrice app
  2. Tap the search bar at the top of the screen
  3. Enter the product name or barcode
  4. Tap the search icon or press enter
  5. View results with prices from different stores
Search Tips
  • Use specific product names for better results
  • Include brand names when possible
  • Use barcode scanner for exact matches
  • Check spelling if no results appear

5. Price Comparison

Understanding Price Results

After searching, you'll see a list of stores with the product. Each listing shows:

  • Store Name: The retail location
  • Price: Current selling price
  • Availability: In stock or out of stock status
  • Distance: How far the store is from your location
  • Last Updated: When the price was last verified
Sorting Options

Sort results by:

  • Lowest Price
  • Nearest Location
  • Availability (In Stock First)
  • Store Name (A-Z)

6. Using Store Locator

Find Stores Near You
  1. Tap the "Stores" tab at the bottom navigation
  2. Allow location access if prompted
  3. View the map with store markers
  4. Tap on a marker to see store details
  5. Tap "Get Directions" to open in your maps app
Store Information

Each store listing includes:

  • Store name and branch
  • Address and contact number
  • Operating hours
  • Distance from your location
  • Current status (Open/Closed)

7. Setting Price Alerts

How to Set an Alert
  1. Search for your desired product
  2. Tap the "Set Alert" button on the product
  3. Choose your target price
  4. Enable notifications
  5. You'll receive a notification when the price drops to or below your target
Managing Alerts
  • View all active alerts in the "Alerts" section
  • Edit or delete alerts as needed
  • Set alerts for multiple products
  • Alerts remain active until price is reached or you delete them

8. Tips & Tricks

💡 Pro Tips
  • Compare Before Buying: Always check at least 3 stores before making a purchase decision
  • Check Stock First: Call the store to confirm availability before traveling
  • Use Offline Mode: Previously searched products can be viewed without internet
  • Set Multiple Alerts: Track prices for items you buy regularly
  • Update Regularly: Keep the app updated for latest features and store data
  • Share Deals: Found a great price? Share it with friends using the share button
  • Save Favorites: Bookmark frequently purchased items for quick access
